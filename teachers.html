Dean

Pedro Penduko is Dean, Reliance Professor of Management and Private Enterprise and Professor of Management at the Midas Academy, and Professor of Political Science at the University of Bangbang. He became Dean of the Midas Academy in 2018, having been a member of the Midas faculty in the Management Department from 1995 to 1997.
